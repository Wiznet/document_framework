## Features

<ul>
  <li>Supports <b>hardwired TCP/IP protocols</b>: TCP, UDP, ICMP, IGMPv1/v2, IPv4, ARP, PPPoE</li>
  <li>Supports <b>Wake-on-LAN (UDP)</b> and <b>WOL over UDP</b></li>
  <li>4 independent sockets with <b>16 KB internal buffer</b> (TX/RX)</li>
  <li>Supports <b>SOCKET-less commands</b> (ARP, PING)</li>
  <li>Auto-Negotiation and Auto-MDIX (10/100 Mbps)</li>
  <li>Ethernet power-down and clock gating for low power</li>
  <li>High-speed <b>SPI interface</b> (Mode 0/3)</li>
  <li>Integrated transformer and RJ45 MAG-JACK</li>
  <li>Compact form factor: <b>23 × 25 mm</b></li>
</ul>
