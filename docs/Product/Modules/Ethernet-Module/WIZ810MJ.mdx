## Features
![](/img/products/wiz810mj/WIZ810MJ.jpg)
<ul>
  <li>Plug-in Ethernet module integrating <b>W5100</b> chip, transformer, and RJ45</li>
  <li>No external hardware design needed for transformer or PHY</li>
  <li>Fast evaluation of W5100 and MCU on target board</li>
  <li>Supports <b>MCU BUS</b> and <b>high-speed SPI interface</b></li>
  <li>Supports 4 independent hardware sockets</li>
  <li>Operating temperature: −40 °C to +85 °C</li>
  <li>Compact and easy-to-use form factor</li>
</ul>
