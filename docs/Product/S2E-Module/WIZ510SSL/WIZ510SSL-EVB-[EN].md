## WIZ510SSL-EVB

![](/img/products/wiz510ssl/wiz510ssl-evb/wiz510sslevb_sideview.png)

  - WIZ510SSL Developer Board.
  - USB to UART chip, CP2104.
  - RESET Tact SW.
  - BOOT0 Slide SW.
  - H/W Trig Slide SW.
  - Expansion GPIO TEST (Digital / Analog)
  - Micro USB.
  - WIZ510SSL-EVB (TTL/232)
      - RS-232C Connector, D-SUB9-MALE.
