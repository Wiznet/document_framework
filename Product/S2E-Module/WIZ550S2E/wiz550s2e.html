WIZ550S2E

WIZ550S2E is a gateway module that converts serial protocol (RS-232/422/485) into TCP/IP protocol. This module enables remote gauging, remote management of the device through the network based on the Ethernet and the TCP/IP by connecting to existing equipment with serial interface. In other words, WIZ550S2E is a protocol converter that transmits the data sent by serial equipment as TCP/IP data type or vice versa.
